An Obstacle Avoiding Robot is an autonomous robot designed to navigate its environment while detecting and avoiding obstacles in its path. It typically uses sensors like ultrasonic, infrared (IR), or LiDAR to detect obstacles and an onboard microcontroller (such as an Arduino or Raspberry Pi) to process sensor data and control movements.

Modal Analysis of Bridge structure

Modal analysis is crucial in understanding the dynamic behavior of structures like bridges. It helps identify the natural frequencies and corresponding mode shapes, which are essential for assessing the structure's response to dynamic loads and avoiding resonance. This study aims to perform a modal analysis of a bridge using ANSYS Workbench to determine its natural frequencies and deformation patterns. .
